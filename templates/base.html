CrowdCare

A crowdfund-and-connect platform for health services on XRPL

Browse Campaigns Doctor Portal Patient Portal Donor Portal

How it works

  • Doctors create invoices for patients and maintain reserve accounts
  • Patients pay directly via XRP or enable crowdfunding
  • Donors contribute to campaigns for patients in need

Trust & Safety

  • Charges posted by verified doctors carry a trust factor
  • Suspicious charges can be reported (crawlback)
  • All payments settle on the XRPL testnet
